The Story of the book

chapter 1

molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. on her desk, lying between the copies of Nature,New Scientist, and all other scientific journals her work had appreared in, was a well thumped copy of On The Road. It had been molly's favourite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls more she felt she needed to be free.

she had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an oscar wilde quote proclaming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Alqthough many consider her pioneering work, unraveling the secrets of the Ilama DNA, to be an outstanding acheivement, molly did think she had something better to do.
